Writing a perfect cold email is an art. Here are 5 ways to ensure your AI-generated emails get the best results:

1. **Be Specific with Skills**: Instead of 'Full Stack Developer', tell the AI about your 'MERN stack expertise'.
2. **Use the 'Why Them' Hook**: Explain why you want that specific company, not just any company.
3. **Keep it Short**: Hiring managers read emails on their phones. 3-4 paragraphs max.
4. **The Confident CTA**: End with a soft but clear request, like 'Would you have 10 minutes for a quick chat?'
5. **Review Before Sending**: MailApply gives you a preview—take 5 seconds to tweak the tone to match your personality.

Personalization isn't just about 'Hi [Name]'; it's about proving you are the solution to their problem.
