The 'Black Hole' of job applications is the ATS (Applicant Tracking System). These bots filter out up to 75% of resumes before a human ever looks at them.

### The Problem with Job Portals
If your resume doesn't have the exact font or the exact keyword density for 'Node.js' or 'Project Management', you're out. It's a game of luck.

### The Solution: Bypass the Bot
When you email a recruiter directly via MailApply, you aren't going through a bot. You are landing in the 'Primary' inbox of a human being. A recruiter's job is to find talent—they *want* to see well-crafted, direct emails from qualified people like you.

Skip the portal, send the mail, and get the interview.
