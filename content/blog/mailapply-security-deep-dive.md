At MailApply, we understand that your professional email is your most valuable asset. That's why we built our platform with security as the foundation.

### We Never Store Your Password
When you connect 'Continue with Google', we use **OAuth 2.0**. This means we never see your password. Google simply gives us a temporary 'token' that allows us to send drafts on your behalf.

### Encryption at Rest
Every piece of profile data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Your resumes and contact lists are stored in isolated, secure silos.

### You Are in Control
You can revoke MailApply's access at any time directly through your Google Account settings. We only ask for the permissions absolutely necessary to deliver your outreach.
