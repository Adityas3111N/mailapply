Are you still applying for jobs by clicking 'Easy Apply' on LinkedIn? You are likely competing with 500+ other applicants within the first hour.

### The Black Hole of Job Portals
Most major companies use ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) to filter resumes. If your resume doesn't have the exact keyword density the bot wants, no human ever sees your name.

### The Direct Advantage
MailApply helps you skip the line. Instead of shouting in a crowded room (portals), you are whispering directly to the decision-maker (the recruiter's inbox). 

**The Math:** 
- 100 Manual Applications = ~2-3 Responses.
- 10 MailApply Targeted Emails = ~3-4 Responses.

Quality always beats quantity. Stop the lottery and start the conversation.
