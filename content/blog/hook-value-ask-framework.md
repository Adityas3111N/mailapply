The secret to a 50% open rate isn't magic—it's structure. At MailApply, our AI is trained on the 'Hook-Value-Ask' framework.

### 1. The Hook
This is your first sentence. It proves you aren't a robot. "I saw your recent post about the expansion of the Engineering team at [Company]..."

### 2. The Value
Why should they care? This is where your skills come in. "Based on my 3 years of experience in React and TypeScript, I can help you scale your frontend architecture..."

### 3. The Ask
What do you want? Don't ask for a job yet. Ask for a conversation. "Would you be open to a 10-minute sync this Thursday?"

By focusing on these three pillars, MailApply ensures your outreach is professional, concise, and effective.
