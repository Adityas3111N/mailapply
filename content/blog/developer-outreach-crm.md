Most developers keep track of their job search in a messy spreadsheet or—worse—in their head. This lead to forgotten follow-ups and missed opportunities.

### Job Search as a Funnel
Think of your job search like a sales funnel:
- **Prospects**: Companies you want to work for.
- **Outreach**: Emails sent.
- **Leads**: Recruiters who reply.
- **Deal**: The Job Offer.

### The Power of the Tracker
MailApply's Tracker feature isn't just a list—it's your command center. You can see exactly which emails were sent, which skills were highlighted, and where each application stands. 

Organization is the secret weapon of the successful job seeker.
