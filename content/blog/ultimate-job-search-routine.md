Job hunting shouldn't be a 40-hour-a-week job. If you use the right tools, you can stay ahead in just 30 minutes a day.

### The 30-Minute Sprints
1. **08:00 - 08:10**: Review the 'New Jobs' list on your favorite board.
2. **08:10 - 08:25**: Use MailApply to send 5-10 highly personalized reach-outs to those new postings.
3. **08:25 - 08:30**: Check your 'History' tab for any replies from yesterday.

### Why this works
By sending a small batch of high-quality emails every single morning, you ensure a steady stream of interview invites throughout the week. 

Success is built in the routine, not the 'all-nighter'.
