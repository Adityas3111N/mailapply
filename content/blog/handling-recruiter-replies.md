Congratulations! A recruiter has replied to your MailApply email. Now what? 

### Speed is Key
Recruiters move fast. If you wait 2 days to reply, they might have already booked 5 other interviews. Try to reply within 4 hours.

### Be Flexible but Firm
If they ask for your availability, give them specific blocks. "I'm free tomorrow from 2 PM to 5 PM or Wednesday morning." This makes it easy for them to just pick a slot.

### Use the History Tab
Need to remember exactly what you said to them? Use the MailApply **History** tab to see the AI-generated context of your first outreach. It will help you stay consistent and professional.
